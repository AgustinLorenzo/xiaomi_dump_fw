What information is collected and how do we use it?

Types of Information Collected

In order to provide our services to you, we will ask you to provide personal information that is necessary to provide those services that you have subscribed or purchased. If you do not provide your personal information, we may not be able to provide you with our products or services as contracted.

We may collect the following types of information including personally identifiable information:

Router information: activation state, binding state, active state, model, system version, MAC address, device SN, device ID, country code, switch state for notification light, switch state for USB 3.0 and the location selection Network information: network SSID, internet mode, gateway address, WAN upload and download speed, encryption mode, Wi-Fi channels, encrypted password, switch state for MU-MIMO, switch for 2.4G/5G and VPN settings.

Terminal equipments information: online and offline behavior and time, device name, IP address, MAC address, device type, device brand, device model, connection type, signal intensity, noise intensity, upload and download speed, max upload and download speed, guest Wi-Fi identification information, time of access and leave guest Wi-Fi, ios/Android, devices’ online time and access network count. When excessive network bandwidth consumption is detected, information relating to such an event including its duration is collected for further performance evaluations.

Location information: country code, GPS information (with your consent), default time zone, default date, default time and default language.

Hard drive information: if you connect an external hard drive to the router, we may collect and use information such as general situation of the pictures and files you stored and backup including the size, count and indexes. Information specific to you that may be assigned by us: we may collect and use information such as your Mi Account ID.

Log information: if you upload log by APP or web admin background, we may collect and use the information such as the suggestions, screenshot(optional), contact info, Mi account, APP version, time you uploaded the log, router information, terminal equipment information, CPU usage and memory usage.

Other information: environmental characteristics value (ECV) (i.e. value generated from Mi Account ID, connected Wi-Fi ID and location value).

How the personal information is used

Personal information is collected for providing services and / or products to you, and compliance under applicable laws.

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

Providing, processing, maintaining and improving our goods and/or services that you have purchased, including after-sales services, customer support or through our websites and while troubleshooting your device.

Storing and maintaining information about you for our contractual or legal obligations.

Analysing and developing statistical information on use of our products and services to better improve our services and experiences to you.

Communicating with you about your device, service or any general queries, such as updates, customer inquiry support, information about our events or notices that you may subscribe.

Here are more details on how we use your information (which may include personal information) –

Providing basic services. Information (activation state, model, country code, system version, MAC address, device SN, device ID, network SSID, internet mode) is collected for activating the routers.

Providing management functions. You can use Mi Wi-Fi APP to manage your network and connected terminal devices. In order to support the service, we will collect and use any or all of the information such as router information, network information, terminal device information, location information and hard drive information. The router-related information is used to identify the device and provide the corresponding functions for each one. For example, the system will check if the router need to enter the initialization process according to the activation state, if the router’s already been bound or should enter the binding process, and the system also have to confirm the default time and language based on the country code of the router. Different system version provides corresponding functions for each router.

These information will be shown in the admin background platform and APP for you to control your network. Network information such as network SSID, non-plain password and hard drive information will also be used for remoting management. When you remote access your profile in the external hard drive connected to the router, the information will go through our server, then sent to your terminal devices, but we will not store the flow in our server. Providing push service. Mi account, terminal device information, disk information and network information will be collected for the purpose of providing push service. Push content including terminal device online reminder, upgrade reminder, backup behavior reminder, Wi-Fi channel switched reminder, router share reminder, system error reminder and function recommend. With push services, terminal device online reminder is used to inform you the connection of new devices or specified devices. Upgrade reminder will be sent to you when there’s a new version. Router share reminder is used to notify you the process of the operation. Wi-Fi channel reminder and system error reminder will notify you the status of your network and system. Function reminder is used to recommend relevant function to help you improve your network.

Collecting user feedback. The feedback you choose to provide is valuable in helping Xiaomi make improvements to our services. We will diagnose and solve the problem by using the relevant data we collected from the log you uploaded. In order to follow up on the feedback you have chosen to provide, Xiaomi may correspond with you using the personal information that you have provided and keep records.

Share administrator privileges. Mi account will also be used for sharing the administrator privileges.

Providing location based service. Location information will be used for providing you the corresponding service and best user experience (e.g. area selection when you login). You have the choice to decide whether to share your location information with us.

Verifying user identity. Xiaomi uses the ECV value to verify the user identity and ensure there is no log-in by hackers or unauthorised persons.

Sending notices. From time to time, we may use your personal information to send important notices, such as communications about changes to our terms, conditions, and policies which you subscribe.

We will not analyze your log or web activities in any way. We respect your personal privacy. We will only use the information that you have provided in meeting our contractual needs or you have agreed or it is required under the applicable laws.
